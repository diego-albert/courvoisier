café Courvoisier®

restaurant & café serve

Café Courvoisier is a perfect after-dinner experience of cognac paired with coffee served with dessert or a chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 50 ml Courvoisier VSOP Cognac

  • 200 ml espresso coffee

Method

Pour Courvoisier VSOP into the glass. Pour espresso coffee into the cup. Present on serving tray with chocolate.

Courvoisier® Espresso Martini

restaurant & café serve

Courvoisier Espresso Martini is a is an original, luxurious take on the legendary cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 35ml Courvoisier VSOP

  • 15ml coffee liqueur

  • 30ml cool fresh espresso coffee

  • 10ml sugar syrup

Method

Shake and fine strain into a coupette glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
