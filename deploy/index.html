Café Courvoisier is a perfect after-dinner experience of cognac paired with coffee served with dessert or a chocolate.

more

Ingredients

  • 50 ml Courvoisier VSOP Cognac

  • 200 ml espresso coffee

Method

Pour Courvoisier VSOP into the glass. Pour espresso coffee into the cup. Present on serving tray with chocolate.
